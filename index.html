High School:

Whhere Dreams go to die and stress comes to life.

-Edwin Acevedo

Did you ever think you would go through highschool as if it were like buying a donut blindfolded? Probably not. Which is because thats exactly wht it's like


Now in order to get through high school I had to struggle, I was broke, stressed and exhausted. The three feelings that are commonly despised. Now, I would hate that the up and coming generation of students will have to struggle as I did. It didn't help either when you ask an adult for advice on what to do and they give you the least fun option for you to go about. It's always along the lines of Do your work, do your homework go to bed early, blah blah blah.


Now I am no top notch executive to tell you how to live your life and what you are supposed to do. Nor am I some role model or life coach that has the goal to inspire the world. But, I am a student. And I have been in your place before. So If nothing else I have said has proven any use to you, then I surely hope the tips I provide can prove themselves useful.
