High School:

Where Dreams go to die and stress comes to life.

- Edwin Acevedo

Exhausted, Stressed, and Broke some of the bigger issues a student has to deal with. Issues that adults think are so simple to solve not understanding that we no longer live in the 90s or the 80's where you can get a job at any age if you kew the right people.


We hear the same things over and over as students. Which honestly is not fun. Adults like to tell us the same old "Do your homework", "do your classwork", "go get a job" etc. We need REALISTIC ADVICE.
